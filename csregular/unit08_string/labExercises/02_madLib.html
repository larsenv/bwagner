MadLib

Directions

A Mad Lib is a funny, often ridiculous story created when you fill in the blanks with the part of speech that is requested. Most word descriptions will ask for simple parts of speech, like a noun, verb, adjective, adverb, and more. Sometimes the description will ask for a body party, a number, the name of someone you know. All these different descriptions give you an opportunity to really be creative.

Write a program that creates a Mad Lib for the theme song to the TV show Gilligan's Island. Start by prompting the user to enter word descriptions with the part of speech indicated that will be used to fill in parts of the song. Once all of the word descriptions have been entered, display the song with the users words inserted.

The song below is marked with numbers indicating where the user's responses will be inserted into the song. [1] indicates the first word entered by user.
